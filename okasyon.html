About Okasyon

No two events are the same. Every project is unique to us, every event story is told in its own special way. For Okasyon, every event is a masterpiece on its own - a vision transformed into a reality.

Rather than event managers, we like to think of ourselves as event architects. We take the time to understand our clients' needs and orchestrate event solutions that are unique from scratch and exceed expectations each time.

VISION

  • To become one of the innovators in the event management industry by providing the most creative and cost-efficient solutions, to design and to create experiences of exceptional quality, surpassing client’s expectations in bringing each and every event from a Vision to a Reality.

MISSION

  • We believe in elevating the standards of each event, by ensuring superior quality services and professionalism, and to promote excellence in all that we do, as we offer trained eyes to every details of the event. We have an unwavering commitment to our customers, being guided by our values, to exceed their expectations in terms of value, service and innovation.
